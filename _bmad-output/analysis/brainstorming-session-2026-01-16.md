_Raison:_ Simplicité immédiate et lisibilité pour le joueur.

## Decision: Level Up Mechanics
**The "Rule of Three" Choice:**
At each level up, the player chooses one card from 3 options:
1.  **Stat Upgrade:** +2 to +3 to a random Stat (Force, Vit, or Speed).
2.  **Weapon:** Grants a random Weapon (New equipment).
3.  **Trait:** Grants a random Trait (Passive bonus).
_Goal:_ Validates the "Grinding" aspect by offering constant meaningful choices.
